Home of the Dragon

Opened in 2002, Dragon's Den provides a modern fine-dining approach to traditional Chinese cuisine.

Dragon's Den is known for its 15-course tasting menu, which also includes a la carte offerings.

Today, we continue to give our guests a taste of China in each and every dish prepared.

Each ingredient used in our kitchen is imported from one of the various provinces of China to ensure authenticity of flavor.

About Us

Since we began life on the planet Earth, we have been exposed to Chinese food from our parents.

Our mission is to be able to share the flavors of Chinese cuisine in an elevated manner and allows diners to experience the same flavors we grew up on.

Here at Dragon's Den, we are committed to providing an elegant and refined dive into the culture of China through food.

We pride ourselves on an upscale dining environment set in the middle of bustling Chinatown.
